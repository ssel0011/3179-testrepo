Alcohol Consumption

By Sujan Sellathurai

Alcohol is consumed all over the world. Being such a popular beverage option, alcohol is vastly consumed and these visualisations provide the summary of it. Here you can see who or to be more specific which country consumes the most alcohol from servings of beer, to wine and then to spirits.

Total Alcohol Around The World

For a brief summary, this map contains the total litres of pure alcohol that is being consumed around the world. Here you can view the total litres of alcohol that has been consumed from each country, noting that it is based on pure concentration of alcohol. You can hover over each country to view this exact number

Top 10 Countries based Different Servings

Now that we get the idea of the amount of alcohol consumed around the world, lets have a look at the different kinds of alcoholic drinks around the world, based on the different beer servings, wine servings and spirits servings Use the selection menu below the graph to change between the types of beverage. You can also over over the bars to view the amount of servings

Top 10 Countries based on Pure Alcohol

Here we can view a better summary of the different servings of alcohol, however, this shows the top 10 countries based on the total litres of pure alcohol and thier different servings. The red cross displays the total litres of pure alcohol, yellow circle shows beer servings, the pink square is spirit servings and the blue triangle is wine servings. You can hover over each point to view the information

Total Litres Against Servings

Now that we have seen a well laid out summary, this visualisation dives into more depth. It shows the different servings based on the one that you pick against the amount of pure alcohol served for each country. Once again you can hover over the different points to view the exact information and use the selection menu to change between the different types of servings
